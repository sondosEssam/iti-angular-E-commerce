About ITI Shop

We craft great e‑commerce experiences. Learn who we are and what we value.

Our mission

To provide a clean, responsive, and accessible shopping experience for students learning modern web development. This project demonstrates best practices for component structure, routing, and UX.

Our story

Founded as an academic project, ITI Shop started as a small idea and grew into a full-featured demo store used for teaching and showcasing development skills. Along the way we focused on clear code, accessibility, and performance.

What we believe in

  • Accessibility — everyone should be able to shop online.
  • Performance — fast pages reduce drop-off and improve conversions.
  • Clarity — easy navigation, thoughtful content.
